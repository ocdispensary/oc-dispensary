About Us

Whether you’re a seasoned enthusiast or new to the world of cannabis, we are here to meet all your needs with our wide selection of premium products, competitive prices, and exceptional customer service. Our goal is to make high-quality cannabis accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone.

Why Choose OC Dispensary?

We know you have options when it comes to purchasing cannabis in NYC, so what makes OC Dispensary stand out?

1. Best Prices in the City

At OC Dispensary, we offer the most competitive pricing in the market. We believe that access to high-quality cannabis products should not break the bank. By keeping our overheads low and sourcing directly from trusted growers, we pass the savings on to you. Whether you’re shopping for flower, edibles, concentrates, or pre-rolls, you’ll find top-tier products at prices that won’t be beaten by any other dispensary in the New York City area.

2. Convenient Delivery and Pick-Up Services

Your time is valuable, and we make it easy to get the cannabis products you need without any hassle. OC Dispensary offers delivery and pick-up services across all of New York City, including Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, Staten Island, and the Bronx. Whether you’re relaxing at home or on the go, we bring the dispensary to you. Simply place your order online, and our reliable team will deliver it right to your door. Need your products faster? Opt for our quick pick-up service, and we’ll have your order ready when you arrive.

3. High-Quality, Curated Selection

We are passionate about cannabis, and it shows in the care we take to curate our premium selection. Every product we carry is hand-picked for its quality, potency, and consistency. From indica, sativa, and hybrid strains to innovative cannabis-infused edibles, oils, and topicals, OC Dispensary has something for everyone. Our knowledgeable staff is always here to guide you through our offerings and help you find the right products to suit your preferences and lifestyle.

4. Trusted, Safe, and Legal

At OC Dispensary, we are fully licensed and compliant with all New York State cannabis regulations. Your safety and satisfaction are our top priorities. You can shop confidently knowing that all of our products are tested for quality and potency, ensuring you receive only the best.

How It Works: Ordering from OC Dispensary

We’ve streamlined the ordering process to make it as simple and convenient as possible. Here’s how it works:

  • 1. Browse Online
    Head over to our easy-to-navigate website to browse our extensive range of cannabis products. You can filter by product type, strain, potency, or even flavor to find exactly what you’re looking for.
  • 2. Place Your Order
    Once you’ve made your selections, place your order through our secure online platform. You’ll receive a confirmation with your estimated delivery time, or you can select pick-up for faster service.
  • 3. Fast and Discreet Delivery
    Our professional delivery drivers will bring your order straight to your door anywhere in New York City. Deliveries are fast, reliable, and discreet. You can track your order in real-time, ensuring you know exactly when to expect it.
  • 4. Pick-Up Option Available
    In a hurry? Select the pick-up option when placing your order online, and we’ll have your products ready for you to pick up at your convenience. No waiting in line – just grab and go.
